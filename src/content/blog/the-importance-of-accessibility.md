Building a website that everyone can use is a fundamental requirement of modern web development. But it's not just about doing the right thing.

## Good A11y is Good SEO

Search engines rely on exactly the same markers as a screen reader to understand your content. Writing semantic HTML, using `aria-labels` appropriately, and ensuring high color contrast improves both user experience and indexability.

Let's build a web that is usable for everyone!
